PHOTO POSTER DESIGN(OFFLINE EVENT)



Description


Embark on a journey of creativity with our offline photo poster design event at our college symposium, "Photo Poster Design Event." Participants should capture unique images based on themes like “Urban Elegance”, “Nature's Poetry”, or “Timeless Traditions” to craft visually stunning posters. Don't miss the chance to turn your captured moments into timeless art!
Submit your designs through the provided forms before February 3rd, 2024.
Let your creativity shine!

Rules


1. The picture must be of jpg or jpeg format.
2.The picture should be submitted only via google forms.
3.Only one submission is allowed per person.
4.The picture can be based on any above three themes only.

Certificates and cash prize for winners will be awarded!!!
